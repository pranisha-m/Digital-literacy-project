Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the Digital Literacy course. It covers five key areas of digital literacy, including digital awareness, professional digital presence, coding and collaboration platforms, email etiquette, and cybercrime awareness.
The aim of this project is to build a strong foundation for safe, responsible, and professional digital participation as a student. It also helps in developing essential skills needed for effective communication and collaboration in the digital world.


Tasks Completed
Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Created a one-page infographic explaining digital literacy, safe internet practices, useful tools for students, and professional online presence.

Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Created/updated professional profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to build an online student portfolio.

Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Completed a beginner coding challenge and created a Google Form quiz on Digital Literacy Awareness.

Task 4 – Professional Email & Etiquette Guide
Drafted two professional emails and created a social media do’s and don’ts checklist.

Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study
Prepared a case study on cybercrime and created a prevention checklist for students.
